The Killer Combination
3 Indicator Strategy
01
📊
Bollinger Bands
The Boundaries
Market ki hadd (Range) batata hai. Upar aur niche ki lines market ki limits hain.
Upar wali line touch → Overbought → SELL signal
Niche wali line touch → Oversold → BUY signal
02
📈
RSI
The Confirmation
Market ki thakan napta hai. RSI batata hai ke market palatne wali hai ya nahi.
RSI 30 ke niche → Thak gayi → Strong BUY confirm
RSI 70 se upar → Overbought → Strong SELL confirm
03
🔊
Volume
The Power — Final Confirmation
Market ki asli taqat — bina volume ke koi bhi move FAKE ho sakti hai.
Volume bar pichli 5 bars se unchi → Bade Players aaye → TRADE ENTER
Low volume par move → Fake breakout → IGNORE
