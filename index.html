The Killer Combination
3 Indicator Strategy
01
📊
Bollinger Bands
The Boundaries
Market ki hadd batata hai. Upar aur niche ki lines market ki limits hain.
Upar wali line touch → Overbought → SELL
Niche wali line touch → Oversold → BUY
02
📈
RSI
The Confirmation
Market ki thakan napta hai. RSI batata hai ke market palatne wali hai ya nahi.
RSI 30 ke niche → Strong BUY confirm
RSI 70 se upar → Strong SELL confirm
03
🔊
Volume
The Power — Final Confirmation
Market ki asli taqat — bina volume ke koi bhi move FAKE ho sakti hai.
Volume pichli 5 bars se uncha → Bade Players → TRADE
Low volume par move → Fake breakout → IGNORE
